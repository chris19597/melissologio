Privacy Policy

App name: Μελισσολόγιο
Package: com.akimos.melissologio
Last updated: May 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Μελισσολόγιο ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: Your email address and password, used to create and manage your account via Supabase authentication.
  • Apiary & hive data: Information you enter about your apiaries, hives, and inspections (e.g. hive health, queen status, honey levels, notes).
  • Location data: GPS coordinates of your apiaries, only when you explicitly provide them. Location is not tracked in the background.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To store and sync your beekeeping records across your devices.
  • To provide the core features of the App (hive management, inspection tracking).

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device (SQLite) and optionally synced to a secure cloud database powered by Supabase. Supabase stores data on servers located in the European Union (eu-central-1 region).

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

5. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and hashed passwords. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
